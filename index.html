Weikang BIAN

I am an M.Phil. student at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, the Chinese University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Wei MENG. Before that, I obtained my B.Eng. degree in Software Engineering from Zhejiang University in 2018.
As a member of the CUHK Computer Security Lab, I work on browser security and dynamic program analysis problems. I am also interested in applying AI to solve traditional security problems.
